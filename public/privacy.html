Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

VeriFunding is operated by ClearMetric LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy describes how we handle information in connection with our commercial financing referral services.

Who we are

VeriFunding is a commercial financing referral service operated by ClearMetric LLC. VeriFunding is not a lender, makes no credit decisions, and does not guarantee financing. Financing is provided by, and subject to approval by, independent funding partners.

Information we collect

When you submit a funding request through VeriFunding (now or when our intake form is live), we may collect:

  • Business information: legal name, DBA, entity type, start date, state, revenue, and deal details
  • Owner information: name, ownership percentage, email, phone, and credit self-assessment
  • Documents you choose to upload, such as quotes or invoices
  • Referral source and dealer or partner information, if provided

We do not currently operate an online intake form on this website. When intake is available, this section will apply to information submitted through that form or by email at requests@verifunding.com.

How we use information

We use submitted information to review your request, assess likely product fit, and route qualified requests to funding partners that match your profile — only with your consent. We do not sell your information.

Sharing with funding partners

With your authorization, we share your information with independent funding partners to obtain financing offers. VeriFunding may be compensated by funding partners on closed financing; that compensation may be reflected in your terms.

Credit inquiries

When you authorize us to do so, VeriFunding and its funding partners may obtain business and consumer credit reports in connection with your application. Initial review may use a soft inquiry that does not affect your credit score. Hard inquiries may occur if you proceed with a specific lender.

Contact

Questions about this policy: requests@verifunding.com
